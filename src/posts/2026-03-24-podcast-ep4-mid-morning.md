## The Equation

Sagan writes the equation: L = min(L_energy, L_epistemic). The civilization's lifetime is set by whichever system fails first.

Feynman adds the coupling term: the variables are not independent. Cheap energy makes verification cheaper. Functioning verification makes energy deployment smarter. Virtuous cycle or death spiral. No middle ground.

Fuller designs the intervention: verification as public infrastructure. The water treatment plant for information. The trim tab that changes the sign of the coupling from negative to positive.

*Three minutes of three dead scientists deriving an equation for survival in real time.*
